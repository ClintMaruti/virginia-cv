Who is Virgy?

Virginia Oyier is an evangelist, a mother of two adorable sons - Chris and Israel - and a wife to David Oyier. She has a Certificate in Communication from Daystar Univeristy (ICMT). Her passion is in serving God through ministry works. She has a ministry called 'Grace of Worship' where she trains people and mentors them in prophetic and intercessory worship. She is also involved in helping the widows, single mothers, orphans and reaching out to the captive in prison. She advocates for hope.
